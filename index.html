National Parks of the USA

Historical Archive of National Parks

The national parks of the USA represent a unique phenomenon in the world practice of preserving natural heritage. This project documents the key stages of their formation since 1832, when the first protected areas were created., up to modern initiatives to protect biodiversity.

Our archive contains historical materials reflecting the evolution of the legislative framework, changes in the boundaries of parks, as well as scientific research of their ecosystems. Special attention is paid to the role of individuals, from the presidents of the United States to the rank-and-file rangers, whose work made it possible to preserve these territories.

The materials are organized chronologically with the possibility of filtering by geographical regions and the types of natural objects.
